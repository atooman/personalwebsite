Who Am I?
I am currently a junior at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering with a minor in User Experience in the School of Information.
Where am I from?
I am from Cincinnati, Ohio and attended high school at Ursuline Academy. Raised a wolverine in enemy terriroty, it has been a dream to be able to attend one of the top public instiutions in the nation.
What have I done?
This past summer, I was fortunate enough to intern at Accumtech were I was able to get a lot of exposure to frontend developement. Due to the smaller nature of the company, I was able to not only work on the code behind the feature, but help with the design. I enjoyed the process of designing a feature and then making it a reality through code. I have significantly enjoyed my experience with frontend developement and design, but I am excited to continue trying new fields throughout computer science to find the one that is best for me.
Why computer science?
I initially chose computer science because of the creativity of the field. I took a class in high school dedicated to app development and fell in love with the idea of constantly being able to innovate and enhance the features of an application I created.
Why still computer science?
As a student at the University of Michigan, I have been exposed to a much more technical side of computer science. I fell in love with this technical component in ENGR 101 where I felt that I was able to learn how code works when it is run. While EECS 280, 203, and 281 were a lot more challenging and pushed my limits, the challenges they provided constantly excited me and gave me the fuel I needed to keep working to find the solution. All aspects of coding excite me, the technical part of understanding the logic behind code and the creative component in designing code for various features.
What am I passionate about?
With the abundance of fields available in computer science, I am eager to learn all of the different avenues to find the one that is the best fit for me and allows me to make a difference. In addition to computer science, I am passionate about working on a team and communicating with others as indicated by my membership in a social sorority and professional fraternity. I enjoy the sense of community and support that both create and love to bring an optimism in the words of our football coach, "unknown to mankind" to every task and team I am a part of.

My Classes

Programming and Introductory Data Structures

In this class, I learned the tools necessary for effective programming and expanded my knowledge of data structures to include pointers, stacks, queues, arrays, and trees. In addition, I learned of inheritance, recursion, and parameter passing methods.

Data Structures and Algorithms

In this class, I further expanded my knowledge on data structures to include lists, priority queues, hash tables, binary tress, and search trees. In addition, I learned of the common sorting and search algorithms as well as the O-notation associated with each.

Discrete Math

This class was an introduction to the mathematical foundations of computer science. In this class, I learned of prepositional and predicate logic, growth of functions, and discrete probability theory.

User Interface Development

I will be taking this class during Fall 2022 semester. I hope to learn about techniques for designing computer system user interfaces easily accesible for the users. In addition, I am excited to learn of task analysis, design of functionality, display and interaction design, as well as usability evaluation.

Introduction to Information Studies

I will be taking this class during Fall 2022 semester. This class will cover the economical, political, and cultural impact of the Information Revolution. It will provide the foundation needed for later study in Information and prompt thoughtful consideration about complex information issues.

The H in HCI: Human Perception, Cognition and Mental Processes

I will be taking this class during Fall 2022 semester. It will explore human capabilities and behavior and how these influence the design of interactive information systems.
